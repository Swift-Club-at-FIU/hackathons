# 305 HackShells

305 HackShells is a 12-hour student hackathon hosted by Swift Club at FIU. It is built for students who want to team up, build a real project, and finish the day with something polished enough to demo.

## Event Overview

305 HackShells brings together students interested in:

- Swift
- iOS development
- Android development
- Google technologies
- Amazon technologies
- UI/UX design
- Frontend development
- Backend development
- AI-powered features
- Product thinking
- Creative technology

Whether you are a beginner or already building projects, the event is designed to help you learn, collaborate, and ship something real in one day.

## Date and Location

- **Date:** Friday, April 10, 2026
- **Location:** Florida International University
- **Room:** To be announced

## Format

- **Length:** 12 hours
- **Style:** One-day hackathon
- **Includes:** team formation, mentor support, building time, submissions, judging, and closing recognition

## Team Rules

- You may join **solo**
- Teams may have **1 to 4 participants maximum**

## Prize

- The **winning team** will receive a **$500 Amazon gift card**
- **Restrictions apply**
- A project must meet **all project submission requirements** to be eligible to be selected as the winner

## What Participants Can Expect

- Meet other students and form a team quickly
- Build during a focused hackathon sprint
- Get support from mentors throughout the day
- Submit a project and present a demo
- Connect with Swift Club at FIU and other student builders

## Event Flow

### 7:00 AM to 11:00 AM
- Check-in

### 11:00 AM to 5:00 PM

- Closing recognition
